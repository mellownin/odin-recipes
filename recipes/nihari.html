Nihari

Description

Nihari is a classic North Indian curry typically prepared with slow cooked red meat such as lamb. In this recipe, we attempt this beloved dish without any meat whatsoever. Prepare yourself for a delicious (and spicy) experience!

Ingredients

  • Nihari spice pack (avaialble at your local indian (or asian) store
  • 4 roma tomatoes
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 medium sized potatoes
  • 1 cup of uncooked pinto beans
  • 2 one inch cinnamon sticks
  • 1 tsp of cumin seeds
  • Vegetable oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Pack of frozen garilc naan

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables and garlic
  2. Heat 2 tsp of oil in a cooking pot
  3. While waiting for oil to heat, throw the pinto beans in a pressure cooker along with a cup and half of water. Cook at high pressure for 10 minutes. Fill a second pot with water and place on the stove; get that bad boy boiling.
  4. Come back to your heated pan and throw in the tsp of cumin seeds
  5. Once you can smell the aroma of the seeds, throw in the garlic, onions, and cinnmon. Stir gently and the veggies sizzle
  6. As the vegetables saute, throw your diced potatoes in the large boiling pot
  7. Once the veggies are sauted, pour the coconut milk over them. Add in the nihari spice powder and throw in the roma tomoatos. Bring this concotion to boil.
  8. Check back in on the potatoes. If you can easily drive a fork through a potato then it's time to drain those fools. Safely isolate the soft potatoes and add them to the pot with everything else.
  9. Once this big pot begins to boil turn off the heat and allow the whole thing to simmer down
  10. As the pot simmers, heat up your garlic naan (either on a pan or in an air fryer) per the packet instructions
  11. Finally, check in on the beans you've been slow cooking. Drain them of any excess water and add them into the curry pot
  12. Mix everything up nice and good and leave to marinate for another 10 minutes
  13. And....you're done! Enjoy this exceptional batch of delicious curry to your hearts content!
