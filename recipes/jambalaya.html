Jambalaya

Description

Jambalaya is a an American Creole and Cajun rice dish of French, African, and Spanish influence, consisting of mainly meat and vegetables mixed with rice.

It's best made spicy and served piping hot. A vegtarian version of the dish can also be prepared by subbing in tofu and vegan sasuage. Just make sure to press your tofu before hand! This page will list out the ingredients and process to constructing the vegetarian variant.

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Jalapeno
  • Cayenne
  • Garlic
  • Creole or cajun seasoning
  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • Extra firm tofu
  • Vegan sasuage
  • Vegetable stock
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • White rice
  • Okra
  • Salt and black pepper
