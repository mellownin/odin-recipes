Jambalaya

Piping hot jambalaya!

Jambalaya is a an American Creole and Cajun rice dish of French, African, and Spanish influence, consisting of mainly meat and vegetables mixed with rice.

It's best made spicy and served piping hot. A vegtarian version of the dish can also be prepared by subbing in tofu and vegan sasuage. Just make sure to press your tofu before hand!
