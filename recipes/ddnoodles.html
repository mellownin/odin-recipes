Dan Dan Noodles

Description

Dan Dan Noodles are one of my favorite dishes. Tastefully spicy and numbingly addictive, I first stumbled upon this dish via my brother's recommendation. Since then, I've looked around for easy to follow recipes available on the web. This page summarizes what an amazing recipe from the Woks of Life. Thank you Judy!

Ingredients (Vegan Variant)

  • Vegan ground meat
  • Noodles of your choice (I like to use Udon)
  • Baby bok choy
  • Schezuan peppercorn
  • Crushed red pepper chili flakes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green onion
  • Star anise
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Shoaxing wine
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Five spice powder
  • Peanut butter or sesame paste
  • Garlic
