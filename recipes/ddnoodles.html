Dan Dan Noodles

Description

Dan Dan Noodles are one of my favorite dishes. Tastefully spicy and numbingly addictive, I first stumbled upon this dish via my brother's recommendation. Since then, I've looked around for easy to follow recipes available on the web. This page summarizes what an amazing recipe from the Woks of Life. Thank you Judy!

Ingredients (Vegan Variant)

  • Vegan ground meat
  • Noodles of your choice (I like to use Udon)
  • Baby bok choy
  • Schezuan peppercorn
  • Crushed red pepper chili flakes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green onion
  • Star anise
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Shoaxing wine
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Five spice powder
  • Peanut butter or sesame paste
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • cinnamon sticks

Steps

  1. Create the chili oil
    • Combine one cup of oil, 1 in piece of cinnamon, 2 star anise
    • Slowly heat combination to 325 degrees Fahrenheit
    • Once at 325 degrees, turn off heat wait 6-7 mins then remove all particles from oil with a slotted sppon
    • Add crushed red pepper flakes and allow them to steep in hot oil
    • Allow oil to cool
  2. Make the "meat mixture
    • Heat up 3 tsp of oil on skillet
    • Once hot, throw in your vegan ground meat and stir till brown
    • During the stir process, add in 2 tsp of hoisin sauce, 2 tsp of shaoxing wine, 1 tsp of dark soy sauce, and 1/2 tsp of 5 spice powder
    • Once complete set aside
  3. Make the sauce
    • Combine 2 tbsp of peanut butter (or seasme paste), 3 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tsp sugar, 1/4 tsp five spice powder, 1/2 tsp sichuan peppercorn (crushed), 1/2 of the prepared chili oil, and 2 cloves of garlic finely minced.
    • Mix ingredients together (note if you've been boiling your noodels at this point and they are ready to go, feel free to use 1/8 cup of hot cooking water from that pot)
  4. Assemble the noodles
